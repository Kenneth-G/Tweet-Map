Tweet Map

About

Tweet Map allows you to enter in a term or hashtag and returns 10 of the most recent tweets which are plotted on the map. This can be used to see how different areas of the world are responding to or tweeting about different tags or trending topics.


Other usages may be for companies who want to see how a product is being received around the world.

Limitations: While the code itself can scale, I have limited the requested amount to 10 to limit Lambda costs.
